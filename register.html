Register Your Vehicle

Connect your contact details with the QR sticker pasted on your vehicle. Registration is required only once.

Why Registration Is Required

Bharat.io works only when a vehicle owner voluntarily connects their contact details to the QR sticker. This ensures that people can reach you quickly during parking issues or emergency situations.

Your details are not visible publicly unless someone scans your QR code. We do not display phone numbers openly on vehicles.

What You Will Need

To complete registration, keep the following details ready. The process takes less than one minute.

  • Your vehicle number
  • Mobile number for parking or emergency contact
  • Email address (optional)
  • Your profession or work details (optional)

Important Guidelines

This QR system is strictly meant for parking and emergency communication. It should not be used for advertising, promotions, or unsolicited calls.

By registering, you agree to share your contact details only for the above purpose.
