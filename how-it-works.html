How Bharat.io Works

A simple QR based system designed to help people contact vehicle owners quickly and safely.

Step by Step Process

1

A unique QR sticker is pasted on the vehicle. Each QR is different and linked to only one vehicle.

2

The vehicle owner scans the QR and registers their contact details once using the form.

3

During a parking issue or emergency, anyone can scan the QR to contact the owner instantly.

Who Can Use This

Bharat.io can be used by car owners, bike owners, parking attendants, residential societies, offices, and public parking spaces. No app installation is required to scan the QR code.

The system works directly through the mobile phone camera or QR scanner.

Designed for Safety

This service is meant only for parking and emergency communication. Contact details are shared voluntarily by the vehicle owner and are not used for advertising or promotions.
