Privacy & Safety

Your privacy and safety are at the core of how Bharat.io is designed and operated.

What Information We Collect

Bharat.io collects only the information that vehicle owners voluntarily submit during registration. This may include a mobile number, email address, profession, and basic vehicle details.

No personal information is collected without the consent of the vehicle owner.

How Information Is Used

Contact details are used strictly for parking and emergency communication when someone scans the QR code placed on the vehicle.

We do not use contact information for marketing, advertising, or promotional purposes.

Data Visibility

Contact details are not publicly listed or searchable. Information becomes visible only when a specific QR code is scanned.

Bharat.io does not display phone numbers openly on vehicles or websites.

Data Protection

Bharat.io does not sell, rent, or share user data with third parties. Data is stored securely and accessed only for providing the service.

We regularly review our systems to prevent misuse or unauthorized access.

Responsible Use

This service is intended only for parking assistance and emergency situations. Misuse, spam calls, or harassment through the system is strictly prohibited.
