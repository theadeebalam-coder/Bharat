Scan QR to Contact Vehicle Owner

Bharat.io is a simple QR-based system that helps people contact vehicle owners instantly in case of wrong parking or emergency situations — without displaying phone numbers on the vehicle.

Register Your Vehicle

What is Bharat.io?

Bharat.io allows vehicle owners to stay reachable without writing their personal phone number on the vehicle. A unique QR sticker is placed on the vehicle, which anyone can scan to contact the owner during parking issues or emergency situations.

Vehicle owners voluntarily connect their contact details to the QR by registering once. After registration, the QR works automatically — no app installation required.

How It Works

1
Register your vehicle by scanning the QR sticker and submitting your contact details once.
2
Paste the QR sticker on your car, bike, or any vehicle in a visible location.
3
Anyone facing a parking issue or emergency can scan the QR and directly call or email you.

Why Use Bharat.io?

✔ No phone number visible on the vehicle
✔ Works on any smartphone camera
✔ No app required
✔ Useful for parking & emergencies only
✔ Owner controls consent

Important Note

Bharat.io is designed strictly for parking assistance and emergency communication. Misuse, spam calls, or harassment is not encouraged and may lead to access restrictions.
