Bharat.io is a simple QR based system that helps people contact vehicle owners instantly in case of wrong parking or emergency. No phone number is displayed on the vehicle.

What is Bharat.io?

Bharat.io allows vehicle owners to stay reachable without displaying their personal phone number on the vehicle. A unique QR sticker is placed on the vehicle, which anyone can scan to contact the owner during parking issues or emergency situations.

Vehicle owners voluntarily connect their contact details to the QR by registering once. After that, the QR works automatically.

How It Works

1

Register your vehicle by scanning the QR sticker and submitting your contact details.

2

Paste the QR sticker on your car or bike at a visible place.

3

Anyone facing a parking issue or emergency can scan the QR and contact you instantly.

Built for Safety & Trust

Bharat.io is designed only for parking and emergency communication. No tracking, no ads, and no data selling. Contact details are shared voluntarily by the vehicle owner.
